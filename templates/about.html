About StripeApp

The most comprehensive subscription analytics and management platform built on Stripe.

Our Mission

We believe subscription management should be transparent, insightful, and empowering. That's why we built StripeApp to give you complete visibility into your subscription lifecycle.

Why StripeApp?

Traditional subscription management tools only show you the current state. We show you the complete journey - every plan change, every proration, every adjustment that led to where you are today.

  • Complete subscription timeline tracking
  • Detailed proration analysis
  • Financial impact insights
  • Seamless Stripe integration

Complete Visibility

Track every aspect of your subscription journey with unprecedented detail and clarity.

What Makes Us Different

Deep Analytics

Go beyond basic subscription data. We analyze proration patterns, plan change triggers, and financial impacts to give you actionable insights.

Real-Time Tracking

Every webhook event is captured and processed in real-time. You'll never miss a subscription change or billing adjustment.

Beautiful Interface

Complex data doesn't have to be confusing. Our intuitive dashboard makes subscription analytics accessible and actionable.
